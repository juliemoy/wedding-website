Staying at Camp Mariah

All guests have the option of staying (for free) in one of many cabins at Camp Mariah for the entire weekend.

The cabins are walkable (on a road, up a hill) from all wedding activities. They have indoor bathrooms, including showers with hot water as well as heat, but no air conditioning. There is plenty of space to have your own quiet area or you can choose to share your cabin with other people you know are coming. There are, however, some disadvantages. Camp Mariah is not a hotel and while all beds have mattresses, they are twin-sized and you will have to bring your own sheets and blankets or sleeping bags as well as towels and anything else you may need. If camping is not for you we have blocked off rooms at two hotels just down the road in Fishkill and will be providing a shuttle bus to and from the ceremony on Saturday. Check out the pictures and do not hesitate to email us with any questions you may have.
